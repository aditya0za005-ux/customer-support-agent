SHIPPING POLICY
We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days) and express shipping (2-3 business days).
Free standard shipping is available on all orders over $50.
International shipping is available to over 50 countries. Delivery times vary by location.
Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number.

RETURN POLICY
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery.
Items must be in original condition and packaging.
To initiate a return, contact support with your order ID.
Once we receive the item, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned.

REFUND POLICY
Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
Processing time is 5-7 business days after the return is received.
If you haven't received your refund after 10 business days, contact your bank first, then reach out to us.
In case of a damaged or incorrect item, we will issue a full refund or replacement without requiring a return.

ACCOUNT AND BILLING
You can update your payment method from the Account Settings page.
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal.
Invoices are emailed automatically after each purchase.
If you were charged incorrectly, contact support with your order ID and we will investigate within 24 hours.

ORDER CANCELLATION
Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement.
After 1 hour, the order may have already been processed for shipping.
If the order has shipped, you will need to return it after delivery.

DAMAGED OR MISSING ITEMS
If your order arrived damaged, take a photo and contact support within 48 hours of delivery.
If an item is missing from your order, contact support with your order ID and we will ship the missing item or issue a refund.

ACCOUNT ISSUES
If you forgot your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
If your account has been locked, contact support to verify your identity and restore access.
You can update your email address from Account Settings. A verification email will be sent to your new address.

PROMO CODES AND DISCOUNTS
Promo codes can be applied at checkout in the "Discount Code" field.
Only one promo code can be used per order.
Promo codes cannot be applied to already discounted items or past orders.
If a promo code is not working, check the expiry date and eligibility requirements.

PRODUCT AVAILABILITY
If a product is out of stock, you can sign up for a restock notification on the product page.
Pre-order items have an estimated shipping date listed on the product page.

CONTACT AND SUPPORT HOURS
Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm EST.
For urgent issues outside business hours, leave a message and we will respond the next business day.
Average response time is under 2 hours during business hours.
